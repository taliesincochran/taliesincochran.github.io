About Me

I'm a full stack developer and former chemist living in the RTP area. I love coding, I didn't think I would, but when I first started I found that I really enjoyed it when I had a challenge and I could build something from scratch to solve that challenge. I love that there is so much to know that I can never run out of new things to learn. I went to the Coding Boot Camp at UNC and learned how to be a full stack developer. I have experience with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, Handlebars.js, MySQL, Sequelize, and Firebase.

When I'm not coding I can be found hiking, cycling, playing with my seven-year-old son, or curled up in my bed reading in my home in Carrboro, NC.
